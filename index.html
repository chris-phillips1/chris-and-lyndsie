CHRIS & LYNDSIE

Why This Website Exists.

I created this website as a way to express my love to the special one in my life, Lyndsie. You are the light of my world, my love. I would not be the person I am today without you. After 2 years of excitement, love, and adventure, I know that you will continue to make life a wonderful experience. This website will always be in flux, with new content and ideas for different celebrations throughout the year.

Currently, the theme of this website will revolve around the concept of music. Since we are both so interested in music, and generally have similar tastes, I thought it might be good to focus on music that connects us and helps to tell our story. Throughout preparation of this website, I have curated a spotify playlist along with a collection of my thoughts listed below.

Our Musical Tastes.

Our relationship began with a shared appreciation for music, specifically, Taylor Swift. While there may be no Taylor Swift on this particular playlist, I think that a lot of the musical styles present here are similar. A lot of this playlist might be classified as "soft pop", but I have also included some other tracks that might deviate from that style of music. These songs are different because they have some lyrical or musical signficance to our story that is explained below. Each and every one of these songs has some sigificance and meaning to me and I hope that you will also see this meaning with time.

Our Songs.

#1: Poetry by Wrabel

Been looking for something / I finally found it right here

Love makes the lightest sound / You know the first time, first time / The first time it comes around

To me, this song is the perfect song to open this playlist with. It really does a good job of expressing how I've felt in this relationship for a while. Specifically, I relate to the idea that I've been "looking for something" and I found it when I met you. The use of a broad term like "something" expresses a sort of ignorance and not necessarily knowing what is being looked for. However, whats important is that I know I have finally found it. And that is our relationship. I have found the love of my life.

#2: Bloom by Adam Friedman

You take my gray sky and turn it into blue / You know you make me bloom

You've planted your love in / The people you've met, oh, oh

This song represents something that we have both struggled with and are both getting better with. I'm referring to the mental health struggles we both face. I think this song illustrates some of what we have. We will support each other in dark times "gray skies" and help make them better. Ultimately, we have both planted love within each other, and it shows in how we operate now.

#3: HOME by WESLEY

Yeah, yea, No matter the road or the distance / You know that home is when I am with you

We could hit the highway / All the places we could see, see, yea, yea

This song seems to focus more on travel/distance and it reminded me of how much long distance we've had to make it through. What I get from this is that no matter where we are, even if it's just a facetime call, when we're together I feel at home. We have shown so much strength by persisting through so many months of long distance. Once we are finally together long-term, we could "hit the highway" and see all the sights we want.

#4: you! by LANY

You found me when I was broken / Put me back together, gave me life

You're the sun to the moon / You're my ocean, painted blue

Another song about mental health. This one help to convey how I've felt since being in a relationship. You truly did find me when I was broken and ready to give up and helped put me back together and come back stronger than ever! I also like the idea that we are as connected as the sun and the moon and that our love is as vast as an ocean.

#5: Come and Go by Juice WRLD

You tell me, ain't nobody better than me, I think that there's better than me / Hope you see the better in me, always end up betterin' me

Saying goodbye to bygones, those are bye, gones / Head up, baby, stay strong, we gon' live long

This song certainly touches on insecurities that I've felt. You have helped me to move past them and understand my own worth. I sometimes questioned whether I was good enough for a relationship, especially with you. Your continuous reassurance, love, and support have helped to feel confident in our love and thrive in our relationship. I also like that the concept of this song is that our love is rare and we have something that most others don't. Whatever we go through, it is worth it.

#6: Perfectly Imperfect by Declan Donovan

So let me give you every color that I find / In your eyes

You're hurting but you're worth it / You don't know why / I would waste my time / But I'm falling and I mean it

This song is more of a reflection on some of the insecurities that we are actively working through in our relationship. What struck me was the second lyric above. While you may not understand why anyone that would want someone like you, I am shocked to see that these thoughts cross your mind in the first place. Our love is not a waste of time, is well worth the effort, and is worth it.

#7: Wait for You by Tom Walker

And if all of this is dragging you under / I'll remind you of the world and its wonder

'Cause when everything is dragging me under / You remind me of the world and its wonder

I like this song just for it's pretty simple message that fits the theme of a lot of the other songs on this list: we are there for each other. When one of us falls down, the other steps in and helps out and the favor is returned when the roles are swapped. We have found good balance in the give-and-take of the relationship. We each offer unique skills to help the other and we utilize them effectively in caring for each other.

#8: The Few Things by JP Saxe

And I know I keep a lot to myself / But still, you're more a part of me than anybody else

You're one of the few things that I'm sure of / You're one of the few things that I know already

This song really does call me out, but it does truly express some of the things that I may not say, but that I certainly feel. I have struggled with expressing my emotions in any sort of constructive way. While we both know that I am unsure of nearly everything, you truly are one of the few things that I'm sure of. And I completely look forward to the day when I can express my emotions fully with you.

#9: Renee's Song by Bazzi

Oh, isn't it beatiful / Watchin' the cards unfold?

Watchin' you dance tonight / So free and full of life

While your name clearly isn't Renee, I found this song so moving that I had to include it. This classic love song really just got me in my feels thinking about the future that we will one day share with each other. Even thinking about how we're doing now, I get so happy knowing that I have you in my life. The lyric, "watchin' the cards unfold", really gets me. It's an embrace of living in the present and enjoying what we have now too!

#10: i'm in love with u, sorry by j'san

And I know that love is just a shout into the void

And I know that the sun will swallow the only earch / We will ever have, and I'm in love with you, sorry

While this song is on the shorter side, I think it still sounds beautiful and conveys what love looks like knowing that the world will end one day. We ponder what future historians will think of our love, but this song takes it even deeper and shows up that life really is fleeting and we gotta appreciate the love that we are able to experience now while we've still got a planet.

#11: Somebody to You by The Vamps

Everybody's tryna be a billionaire / But every time I look at you, I just don't care"

But now, I wake each day / Looking for a way that I can see your face

I figured I had to have one classic, catchy, pop song in this playlist. So here it is! Honestly though, you've given me a reason to keep moving, to wake up every morning and try to love you the way you love me. You've shown me what true love, kindness, and trust are and how they can be done. I will spend every morning away from you looking for that way to see your face and feel your love.

#12: Nightlight by ILLENIUM

And when I fall apart, promise in the dark / You'll be my nightlight, ooh

Even when I'm in so deep / It gets hard to see, you're still all in

We're always gonna be there for each other. That's what this song reinforces for me. While we may have our conflicts or our struggles together, we will still help each other through the darkness. We both provide light when all the other person can see is darkness. We contrast in ways that ensure we are never alone in the darkness of life.

#13: Million Days by Sabai

Sunday mornings and midnight lights / Turn into songs that you watch me write

Stole your heart and I got lucky / Stole your heart and it was mine / In a million ways / For a million days

Listening to this song, I imagined it from your perspective. I imagine some time in the future where we are having our weekend adventures and you're writing songs about these adventures. I also have some humor in recognizing the lyric, "Stole your heart and I got lucky". We both know I was completely oblivious in the beginning and it really was luck that we did find out we both liked each other. Now we have each others' hearts for a million days and more.
